The Challenge

Designing the Future

This collaborative project between Hyundai and The Design Lab at UCSD aimed to apply human-centered design process to create the foundation upon which future autonomous vehicles would be based on. I am unable to share anymore of this secret information about the project, so if you are interested in learning more, reach out to me!

To succeed our project goals were

  • Discover user needs in autonomous vehicles
  • Develop design solutions based on found needs
  • Prototype and test possible solutions

Project Overview

Duration

November 2018 - March 2019

Objective

Research and develop in-transit activites and amenities for autonomous vehicles.

Responsibilities

Manage undergraduate team, conduct research, develop prototype, delegate tasks, plan and manage project, communicate with Hyundai
